    During the end of 2014, and going into 2015, I have been working on the course creation and maintenance (CCAM) process. This has involved updating our course descriptor and a massive aesthetic move to a modern, Bootstrap-based design.

    Over the summer and autumn of 2014, I focussed on the undergraduate admissions process in the University, particularly the management of applications by our admissions team. With a colleague, we overhauled the design and functionality of the portal.

    For the first half of 2014, I worked on bringing Path (a site I helped developed as a student) into the University's corporate infrastructure and making it available to the whole student population for all of the 5,000 courses available each year.
